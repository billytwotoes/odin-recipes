Flautas

These are flautas. There is chicken inside of them.

Flautas are rolled tacos filled with meat, usually chicken.

Due to the deep-frying of the taco, it has a satisfying crunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 white onion, diced
  • 3 cups shredded chicken
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon mexican oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups oil, for frying
  • Avocado, garnish
  • Cilantro, garnish
  • Creama Mexicana, for serving
  • Salsa, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a medium skillet, add olive oil, onion, and chicken. Cook until onions soften, 3-4 minutes. Then add seasonings and remove from heat.
  2. Heat fry oil in a high-walled skillet over medium heat. Working with one tortilla at a time, fill with a about 1/4 cup of filling. Roll the tortilla into a tight cylinder. Place the rolled flauta in the hot oil, seam-side down so it seels.
  3. Continue to roll the flautas as you fry the flautas until you run out of filling. Fry the flautas for about 2 minutes per side until they are golden brown.
  4. Remove flautas from the hot oil and let drain on a few paper towels.
  5. Serve flautas with avocado, cilantro, creama, and salsa.
