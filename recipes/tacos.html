Tacos

This is what a taco should look like

Tacos are the food of kings, gods, celestial beings, and the forgotten ones.

To consume a taco is an experience that is not forgotten, it is life changing.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lb Flank Steak
  • 1/4 Cup Lime juice
  • 2 Tbsp Chopped cilantro
  • 2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp White Pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp Mexican Oregano
  • 12 Corn tortillas
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • Cilantro chopped

Instructions

  1. Combine lime juice, 2 Tbsp chopped cilantro, minced garlic, White Pepper, Cumin, Chili Powder, Mexican Oregano, and flank steak in large ziploc and let marinade 30 minutes to overnight.
  2. Dice the onion and chop the cilantro. Cover and set aside.
  3. Start grilling the Carne Asada on a greased grill, 6-8 minutes per side
  4. While the meat is resting for 10 minutes, heat up a comal, cast iron skillet or large non-stick skillet over medium high heat.
  5. Dice the meat into small bite size pieces.
  6. Turn the comal/skillet down to medium. Heat up the tortillas, 2 at a time. Heat for approx. 20 seconds on side one, flip and cook for 15 seconds on side two.
  7. Transfer the two tortillas and layer and start building the taco: fill with carne asada and top with onions and cilantro to taste.
  8. Repeat steps 6 and 7 as needed.
  9. Reach nirvana.
